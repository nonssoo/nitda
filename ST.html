Software testing and certification

Services >Software testing and Certification

As part of its regulatory duties, the Agency sought an end to the huge financial losses experienced by both corporate and individual organisations in software procurement through the establishment of a quality assurance laboratory that will enable buyers authenticate software goods during the procurement process. The aim is to encourage the implementation and deployment of indigenous software for proper adoption to meet users’ needs. Objectives To encourage the development of standardized local software for sustainable economic growth. To free Nigeria of pirated and substandard softwares Improve quality in the software industry
